About Me

I’m Mahesh Konda, a passionate software developer currently pursuing my Master's Degree at Oklahoma Christian University, Oklahoma. My journey into tech began with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science Engineering from Aurora's Technological and Research Institute, Hyderabad, India, where I developed a strong foundation in technology With expertise in frontend development and a drive to create impactful solutions. I enjoy working on innovative projects that challenge me to think creatively and solve problems. Collaboration and continuous learning are at the core of my development journey, and I’m always eager to work with teams that push the boundaries of what's possible. Starting with HTML and CSS, I quickly grew my skills across various web and software technologies. I am now focused on contributing to impactful projects and further honing my expertise.
